about Generative? Poems

Generative? Poems is an experimental, work in progress, website that pulls random poems from Poemist via the Poemist API.
It seperates each poem by the author's line breaks and creates drop down menus for each with, the selectable options for each drop down menu are the seperated words from each line of the poem.
In the column to the right of this is listed all of the poems that are displayed above. In the right most column are buttons that allow you to interact with the Generative? Poem.
