VARIABLE LOGO

extra 1

A friend of mine in GD made a variable logo for himself using processing.

When he showed it to me I told him he should try making it in Glyphs 3 because it looked pretty straight forward.

He said he had enough work on his plate as is, and didn't need any more.

In trying to persuade him into giving it a try, I accidentally talked myself into wanting to make it.

Turned into a single glyph with 32 masters.

It took a full day to make, but in the end it looks great, and he got me a nice bottle of white wine as payment.
